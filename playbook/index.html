The Auction Market Playbook: Two Setups, No Guessing

Most traders trade every breakout without checking one thing first: is the market balanced or not? This is the model I trade.

  • Balance vs imbalance, and why you check it first
  • The three filters: market state, location (LVN/POC), order flow
  • Trend setup for when price leaves value, best in NY session
  • Reversion setup for failed breakouts back to the POC, London and thin tape
  • Exact entries, stops, targets, plus two trades broken down
Model 1, Trend

Out of balance, seeking new value

Wait until price clearly leaves value, then ride continuation into the next balance POC. Best in the New York session.

Model 2, Mean Reversion

Failed breakout, back to balance

Fade the breakout that can't hold. Price snaps back to the POC where all the volume sits. London and thin conditions.
